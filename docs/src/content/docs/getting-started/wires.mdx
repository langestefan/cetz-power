---
title: Wires and elbows
description: Straight and L-shaped routing between anchors.
---

import Snippet from '../../../components/Snippet.astro';

Use `wire(a, b)` to connect two points with a straight line, or
`elbow(a, b, corner: "h")` for an L-shaped routing that goes
horizontally first, then vertically (use `corner: "v"` for the
opposite).

<Snippet name="getting-started-wires-elbows" />

Wires aren't full "symbols" — they have no label and no style family of
their own; their stroke follows the global `powergretz.wire.stroke`. If
you want a label on a wire, attach it to the symbol on either end
instead.

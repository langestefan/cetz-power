A real-world wind-turbine single-line: the external grid on the left
feeds a step-down transformer, reactive compensation sits at the tower
base (*mastvoet*), and the induction generator lives in the nacelle
(*gondel*). Every bus is annotated with a voltage and flagged with
the active/reactive power flowing into each of its connections — the
conventional way to read a load-flow result.

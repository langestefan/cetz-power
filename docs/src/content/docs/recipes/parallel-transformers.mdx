Two transformers in parallel between a 150 kV bus (HS1) and a 10 kV
bus (MS1), set to **different tap positions** (T1 at trap −8 / 87 %,
T2 at trap −9 / 98 %). The load-flow result is shown on each side of
each transformer: the unequal taps cause the active and reactive
loading to split unevenly between the two parallel paths even though
the total served load is just one 60 MW + 60 Mvar feeder.
